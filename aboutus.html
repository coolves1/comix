Here's my story

I have grown up reading comic books and collecting trading cards. Which can be said for a lot of people arond the world. What this site does is, it makes it accesible to people all over the world. This site is a safe heaven for people who just want a cool nice community to go to where they can nerd out about comics. I know how hard it can be to connect with people through comics. This site makes it a lot easier to find people to nerd out with on something you truly love.
